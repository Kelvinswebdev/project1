Undergraduate Fees

AppCode's academic year consist of two semesters running respectively from late August to December, and January to May. The fees listed below are per each semester, and also cover textbooks, internet services, other learning material and activity, and support services such as career services, health services, and counselling. For first-year and senior-year classes, fees also support limited funding for student business ideas.
